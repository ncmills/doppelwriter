## 9. OpenAI Usage Study Data (Sept 2025, NBER)

Based on analysis of **1.5 million ChatGPT conversations**:

### Overall breakdown:
- **Practical Guidance**: ~29% of all usage (constant)
- **Writing**: Declined from 36% (Jul 2024) to 24% (Jul 2025) of total usage
- **Seeking Information**: Grew from 14% to 24% of total usage
- **Writing at work**: 40% of all work-related messages

### Writing sub-categories (in order of frequency):
1. **Editing or Critiquing Provided Text** (largest - ~2/3 of all writing messages)
2. **Personal Writing or Communication**
3. **Translation**
4. **Argument or Summary Generation**
5. **Writing Fiction**

### Key insight for landing pages:
**Two-thirds of writing usage is editing/improving existing text, NOT generating from scratch.** This means landing pages should emphasize "improve my writing" and "rewrite this for me" as much as "write this for me."

### Personal vs. work:
- **70% of ChatGPT usage is non-work (personal)**
- Both personal and work usage growing over time
- 3 out of 4 conversations focus on "practical guidance, seeking information, and writing"
